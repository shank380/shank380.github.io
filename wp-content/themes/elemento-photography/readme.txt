Elemento Photography is a Green Turtle Lab Elemento child theme with a unique slider homepage and parallax template

== Description ==

Elemento Photography is a gutenberg compatible child theme of the stunning Elemento Photography theme. is a multi-purpose photography theme that is perfect for building any type of site that requires bold imagery and beautiful photo galleries. It is perfect for professional photographers, photo bloggers, travel websites, portfolio websites, business websites or any or website where your galleries and images need to impress visitors. Large, bold images will have your site visitors in awe and an included parallax pre-made template will add visual interest to your home and landing pages. The responsive layout is optimized for all devices. It’s a fast, SEO-friendly that plays nicely with Elementor and all other visual builders.


== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Type "Elemento Photography" in the search field and press "Enter" on your keyboard
3. Click the "Install" or "Activate" button to use your new theme right away.
4. In the wordpress admin panel click on "Appearance > Customize"
5. Customize the settings on your new website
